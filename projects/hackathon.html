ASEAN-INDIA Hackathon - Winning Project

Project Overview

My team of six students won the ASEAN-INDIA International Hackathon 2021. We developed a comprehensive solution package for local fishermen, providing data-driven tools and insights to improve their operations and decision-making.

I was responsible for the data analysis and visualization components. I worked extensively with ArcGIS to create interactive maps that provided fishermen with actionable geographic and environmental information.
