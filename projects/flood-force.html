Flood Force - Strategic Flood Management Game

Game Development | Pygame | AI in Gaming | Environmental Education

Project Overview

Flood Force is a strategic tile-based flood management game developed as part of the AI in Gaming course at THD. Players take on the role of a flood management specialist tasked with protecting riverside communities from flooding using a combination of infrastructure and natural solutions. The game educates players about sustainable flood management while providing an engaging strategic experience with real-world applications.

My Role

I designed and developed the complete game from concept to implementation, including game mechanics, physics simulation, AI systems, user interface, and educational content. The project involved researching real-world flood management strategies, implementing dynamic water physics, and creating an intuitive gameplay experience that teaches players about climate adaptation and flood resilience.

Technologies Used

Python Pygame Game Design Physics Simulation AI Systems

Key Features

  • Grid-based environment (20x16 tiles) with dynamic water physics
  • Strategic placement of barriers and vegetation for flood control
  • Resource management system with limited budget
  • Two-phase gameplay: Planning phase and Weather/Flood simulation phase
  • Multiple difficulty levels with varying challenges
  • Educational narrative teaching real flood management principles
  • Interactive simulation with immediate feedback
  • Published on itch.io with 56 views and 14 browser plays

Challenges & Learning

The main challenge was balancing educational content with engaging gameplay mechanics. I had to research real-world flood management strategies and translate them into intuitive game mechanics that players could understand quickly. Implementing the water physics simulation required careful consideration of performance while maintaining realistic flood behavior. The project deepened my understanding of game design principles, physics simulation, educational game development, and the importance of user feedback in iterative design. Player validation surveys showed high scores (4-5/5) in playability, game experience, and climate change awareness.

Impact & Recognition

The game successfully addresses climate change education by teaching players about flood management in an interactive format. It has been played 14 times on itch.io and received positive feedback in validation surveys, with players rating it highly for climate awareness (5/5), positive emotions (5/5), and goal orientation (5/5). The project demonstrates the potential of serious games for environmental education and sustainable urban planning.
