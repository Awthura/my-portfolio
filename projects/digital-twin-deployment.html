Enterprise Digital Twin System Deployment

Production AI Systems | Computer Vision | Large-Scale Deployment | Sentics GmbH

Current Role: AI/ML/Deployment/Software Engineer at Sentics GmbH

Project Overview

As an AI/ML/Deployment Engineer at Sentics GmbH, I am responsible for the end-to-end deployment of enterprise-grade digital twin systems at customer sites. These systems utilize large-scale camera networks (8-160 cameras) for real-time spatial intelligence and computer vision applications. I handle the complete deployment lifecycle from camera calibration to production monitoring, ensuring reliable operation of mission-critical AI systems in industrial and commercial environments.

Key Responsibilities

  • End-to-end deployment of digital twin systems at customer sites across various industries
  • Camera network setup: orienting and calibrating 8-160 cameras per installation
  • Manual calculation and optimization of camera extrinsics for accurate 3D spatial mapping
  • Configuration management: creating and maintaining config files for cameras, servers, and DeepStream pipelines
  • AI model lifecycle management: data collection, model training, and deployment
  • Complete software stack implementation: RTSP stream processing to Kafka messaging
  • System integration with advanced data fusion systems
  • Production monitoring and maintenance: ensuring 24/7 system uptime
  • Rapid troubleshooting and system recovery when issues arise
